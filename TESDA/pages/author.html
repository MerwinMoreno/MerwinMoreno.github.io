He is Merwin Jasper Moreno, 19 years old. A student of Polytechnic University of the Philippines, currently taking a Diploma in Information Communication Technology. He has a deep passion in arts and music. He started his journey as an artist when he was 10 years old. He started making drawing about his favorite Pokemon and cartoon characters with the guide of his mother who was also gifted with the same talent. He was able to do more than his mother can do but he always look back to his original mentor and his greatest critique. He also became part of artist organization like Guhit Pinas and became the president of the visual arts club in St.Paul College - Father Louis Chauvet Foundation during his senior years.

Now, Merwin Moreno is investing skills and knowledge that would give him an edge when he started his career. Currently he is taking a short course on TESDA, it is Creative Web Design and Web Development. He believe that it doesn't matter if your are going slow as long as you are making progress. He doesn't like things to be rush he would rather think a hundred times before making a decisions.
